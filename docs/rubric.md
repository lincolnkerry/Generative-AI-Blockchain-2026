# Term Project Rubric (2026)

This rubric follows the Term Project Detail in L1_BuildingAI-Butler.pdf, pages 22-24.

| Category | Points | Evidence expected |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Practicality | 35 | 7-day real-use log, clearly stated problem, working prototype, user-facing value |
| Cost economy | 15 | Monthly cost estimate, local/cloud split, two-tier stack discussion |
| Privacy and security | 10 | Data-flow diagram, threat model, local-first/private-data handling |
| Differentiation vs Big Tech | 10 | At least three concrete advantages over big-tech assistants/platforms |
| Technical rigour | 10 | Architecture, experiments, and analysis using `I = M x HBM x R` where applicable |
| Smartening | 10 | At least one Week 11 smartening method with measurable improvement or clear rationale |
| Documentation and demo | 10 | README, paper/slides, reproducible setup, demo video under 5 minutes |

Total: 100 points.

## Notes for teams

- Claims should be backed by logs, screenshots, measurements, source code, or demo evidence.
- Practicality is weighted most heavily. A small working system used for seven days is better than a large plan that was not tested.
- Cost, privacy, and differentiation should be specific to your project, not generic statements.
